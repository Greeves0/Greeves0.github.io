Limbus Company

This is the third and for now final installement in the franchise. It takes what we've learned about the world that we find ourselves in and makes us explore it.

The LORE

In this game, we follow a rag-tag group of 12 sinners who are bound by contract to an amnesiac manager, a guy with anger issues and an incompetent but earnest bus driver. The game is seperated in cantos, just like Dante Alighieri' Divine Comedy, with each canto exploring a characters backstory that is based to some extent on the litterary counter part. ie: canto VI which is centered around the character of Heathcliff is based on Wuthering Heights, taking the story and remodeling it into something new yet grounded, offerint a modern interpretation of the story while adapting it to the Project Moon universe.

The Gameplay

Limbus Company is a turn based RPG where you can bring a set amount of sinners along with you to battle, that number being anywhere between 5 and 7 currently. To aid in battle, these sinners can adorn Identities that amount to alternate versions of themselves with different skillsets and powers, that come from so called "mirror worlds", in which they obviously have taken different paths in life leading them to not being with Limbus Company. You will get 2 different types of battles in which to use your sinners: chain battles and focused encounters. Chain battles will have you drag a line across the screen to choose the skills you want to use for that turn and are usually quite simple, mostly requirering little thought process to win, at least in the earlier, simpler cantos. On the other hand you have focused encounters that require you to carefully choose what to attack with each individual skill and these battles types are where the gameplay shines the most as they usually require careful consideration and many moves of anticipation, taking into account your status application, damage dealt/recieved and skill rotation.

While it didn't seem like it was the case when I introduced Lobotomy Corporation, it is a very story rich game that delves deeply into the themes of human conciousness, what it means to live and how goals and ambitions can strip you from your humanity, however noble your goal may be.

Lob Corp was founded by Ayin and Carmen with the hope to give hope in humanity and to achieve that goal, they needed energy, lots of it, to fuel their "seed of light" whose goal was to spread hope to the inhabitants of the city

To reach that goal, they setup a laboratory in the Outskirts, out of reach of the watchfull eyes of the head in order to experiment in peace. Their experiements led to the discorvery of Cogito, a substance that could morph humans into abnomalities and although those creatures existed before the meddling of L corp, such as with the Tearful thing that is now the K corp singularity, Cogito made it possible to mass produce said abnomalities.

I have odd cosmic thoughts every day

All research however cannot go well and Carmen committed suicide after one of the children in the facility died in her place with Ayin then putting her in a vat and using that to extract Cogito

Upon reaching day 50, you have gathered enough enkephelin for the seed of light to go off with the entire facility crumbling, trapping abnomlities and employees alike inside.

Library of Ruina

Library of Ruina is a deck building rpg and the second installement in the Project Moon universe that greatly expands upon the previously established universe

Fourth cool button

The LORE

This game takes place in The Library, a massive structure that appeared atop the ruins of the newly defunct L Corp. This library was built by Angela and serves as her way of gathering all the knowledge in the world through inviting many people into the library by promising great riches should you enter and then turning them into books upon being defeat, therefore concentrating their entire life experience into something consumable.

The Gameplay

As evoqued previously, this is a deck building rpg. You gather new cards by defeating those invited to the library, turning them into books and then being able to force their attributes upon you in order to fight potentially stronger foes. The game is dividing in multiple chapters, with each one corresponding to a threat level of the library, randing from urban myth to start of the city. Each level with make you face stronger and stronger foes until you reach the top. To assist you in this goal, you can get more librarians to fight with you and also equip abnomality powers, the very same abnomalities you were "taking care of" in the previous game.

Info rundown

What?

Project Moon is a South Korean game developement studio founded in late 2016 by fourth year university student Kim Ji-hoon

Who?

Kim Ji-Hoon is a massive fan of litterature and pop culture and he founded Project Moon while in university with a few other people as part of the video game developement club. His passion for litterature transpires in all of his games as they are deeply tied to those universes.

Inspirations?

Kim Ji-Hoon is a big fan of litterature and all his games have many ties back to it, may it could just for character names all the way to full on themes and ideas that come from popular books. Example being Heathcliff who obviously comes from Wuthering Heights.

Cultural impact

Project Moon has cultivated a rather small but very passionate fanbase with memes that can be found in virtually every other community, with these individuals being refered to as "sleeper agents" for the way they appear out of thin air, should anything project moon related be referenced.

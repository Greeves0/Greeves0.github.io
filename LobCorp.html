The LORE

This game takes place in Lobotomy Corporation aka L Corp, one of the 26 wings of The City. As with every Wing, L Corp holds a singularity and their own allows the usage of Cogito, an immaterial substances that comes from the collective human experience. This being the city however, said human experience is filled with pain, sorrows and wrath. This cogito is injected into subjects, should the test subject have a strong enough mind, they will be consumed by it and morph into an abnormalities. An abnormalities is an immortal entity that can posses varying degrees of sentience and power but is also able to produce enkaphelin, which is very clean an efficient energy, under certain conditions. These abnormalities are then categorised based on their energy production levels ranging from ZAYIN to ALEPH with ALEPH being the best but also tends to be the most dangerous. The management of these entities represents the gameplay of Lobotomy Corporation.

The Gameplay

As explained previously, the aim of the game is to manage these abnomalities to get as much enkephelin as possible. The game is divided into 50 different days with each day requirering more enkephelin than the last while also providing you with more and more abnos to manage. To manage them, you have your very own employes that will most likely die a gruesome death at the hands of an eldritch abomination (shoutout apocalypse bird). However to minimise the chances of such death, you can level them up and procure them with E.G.O gear. This gear comes in the form of weapons and suits taking on the abnomalities traits and powers, empowering the employe possessing them and allowing them to dish out much more damanage. this damange is very much needed when an abnomality breaches containement, usually when it's cliphoth counter reaches 0.

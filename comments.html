About the community

This game and the project moon universe as a whole posseses a dedicated, almost cult like, fanbase that will take any opportunity to talk about it, the existence of this website being a good example of such behavior. In fact, project moon fans can be found so often that we have been given the name of sleeper agents where we will appear in any comment section or thread, should even a line of dialogue appear within said post, to shitpost (see the genshin wild hunt post).
Apart from the shitpost however, you will find a lot of poeple that just want to talk about one of their favorite games which can lead to very cool interactions. Moreover, you can find a good amount of youtube videos talking about the lore and gameplay, in fact there is a plethora of videos online that do a way better job at explaing how the game works than the tutorial.

The memes and references

This game's community, just like the Project Moon one has many inside jokes and memes that almost all obscure and esoteric that you can only really understand if you are in the community yourself. Some of the memes however are very much in the realm of shitpost with no explanation being available and you have to make your own.
Once you've spent enough time looking at memes, you'll develop a strong sense of pattern recognition and end up seeing the game anywhere.
